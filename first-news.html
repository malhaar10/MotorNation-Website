Mini Countryman E John Cooper Works Pack Launched in India

Published: June 8, 2025 By Nihad Raoof

Mini India today confirmed the introduction of the new Mini Countryman E John Cooper Works (JCW) Pack to the Indian market. The all-electric model will be available in a limited series of 20 units.
